Research & Publications

Using Autorefraction Data to Predict Strabismus with Deep Learning

AAPOS 2024 — Co-author, Machine Learning Researcher

Built a DenseNet-121–based model to detect pediatric strabismus from Spot Vision screener data. Preprocessed 433 charts, handled class imbalance with weighted loss, and evaluated performance using ROC/AUC (AUC ≈ 0.94). Designed a HIPAA-compliant ETL workflow adopted by clinical staff.

Effect of Roundup on Planarian Locomotion

IEEE ISEC 2020 — First Author

Investigated glyphosate’s impact on freshwater planaria locomotion as a bioindicator of ecosystem effects. Designed experimental setup, quantified behavior via grid-based tracking, and analyzed dose effects.

Intermediate Filaments in Human Extraocular Muscles

Umeå University, Integrative Medical Biology — Research Intern

Performed immunohistochemistry and fluorescence microscopy on human EOM tissue to examine desmin, synemin, and tenascin distribution at the myotendinous junctions. Contributed to protocol optimization and qualitative analysis of staining patterns.
